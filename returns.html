If a find did not earn its keep, send it back. Thirty days, no restocking fees, no guilt-trip survey on the way out.

Home/Returns

The short version

  • 30 days from delivery to start a return
  • Item should be unused and in its original packaging
  • No restocking fees, ever
  • Refunds land within 3 to 5 business days after we receive it

Before you ship anything, message us

A quick note to hello@snagnest.com or our contact page usually solves it faster than a return:

  • Wrong size or variant: we send the right one, no charge.
  • Damaged in transit: free replacement, no need to ship the old one back.
  • Not as described or defective: we replace or refund at our cost.

How to start a return

  1. Email hello@snagnest.com within 30 days with your order number.
  2. We reply with a prepaid label and simple instructions.
  3. Drop it in the mail.
  4. Your refund is issued within 3 to 5 business days of it arriving back.

A couple of honest notes

For hygiene reasons, a few categories (anything food-contact once opened) are final sale, and those are clearly marked on the product page. Gift cards are non-refundable. Everything else, we will make right.

Questions about whether something qualifies? Just ask. Every message is read by a human.
